New Generation Health Center: A San Francisco Youth Health Clinic

New Generation Health Center (NGHC) is a youth clinic in San Francisco that helps provide quality reproductive health care for and education about safe sex, pregnancy tests, and STD transmissions.

The Objective

To create a modern website and mobile friendly and responsive website

The Research

I collaborated with another designer on this project. We discovered that a majority of the patients were primarily People of Color (Asians, Hispanics/Latinos, and African American) female. These are patients who have insurance (private/public) who wishes to use NGHC’s services for confidentiality purposes. We also discovered that the user’s primary goal is to find information on contact (hours, location, phone number), information about STD/HIV prevention and contraceptive method to pregnancy reduction.

The Problem

The website is outdated and need to look more modern to stay up to date with the current trends. Through our research, we discovered that their web platform was not mobile friendly for their target audience, who are primarily teens that access their services through mobile devices.

The Solution

We explored and introduced our problem-solving process through creative thinking exercises and collaborative discourses. This helped develop our conceptual ideas to visually express the key messages and objectives of what the clinic wants to convey as a whole. Every choice, from layout and color to type size was to attract the young generation’s attention and the clinic’s image.

We presented our initial concepts as the forefront of the website through the creation of User Analysis, Content and Features List, Sitemap, and Wireframes. This stage is crucial because we want to fully understand the user and their needs. It is also important to identify the problems and how we, as the designers, can solve it through the extensive design process.
