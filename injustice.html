Injustice 2

Mobile App Design

Objective

Updating new design feature for Injustice 2 mobile app game

Assignment

1. A visual representation of the player in the game
  • a) A player can select from various choices to represent themselves in the game
  • b) Player’s username
2. Gameplay related stats (The particular stats chosen are not significant to the design and can be treated interchangeably)
  • a) Number of wins
  • b) Total time played
  • c) Game completion stats in percentage
3. Call to actions
  • a) Button to close the menu
  • b) Button to access “Settings” (You do not need to create a Settings screen)
  • c) Button to access “Help” (You do not need to create a Help screen)

Design Approach

I came up with several different design approaches.

Solution 1 (Final Version)

  • a) I allowed the player to quicly access their character's gameplay with several different tabs for the equipment, abilities, taletns and stats all in one screen.
  • b) In the Equipment, the player is able to access all of their gears all in one go and the players can scroll down to pick out their equipment. Unlike the original game, the player had to click on each equipment icon that took them to another page and doesn't have a scrollbar.
  • c) In the original game, the player is able to create their battle team but in order to do that, the player has to click on the back buttom to switch characters on their team. I thought that that wasted alot of time, so I decided to approach it a different way and add the create team option on the bottom of their selected character and if they already have their team picked out they can simply swap out the characters without having to backtrack.
  • c) Finally, I added the "Create Loadout" button where the players can save mulitpled loadouts for their character's selected gears that way they can switch their gears freely as they collect more gears.

Solution 2 (Second Version)

  • a) This was is pretty much the same idea, but the selected character is in the middle along with his other two teammates faded in the background. The player can simply use their finger to swipe left of right to change the character.
  • b) On the left panel, shows the tab for Awards, Talents, and Stats and their levels.
  • c) On the right panel, it shows the stats for Batman when he is wearing the protective gears and equipment. The player is also able to create multiple gear loudouts.

Solution 3 (First Version)

  • a) This one is very much the same as the ones above except this one creates a character filter where the player can choose to be a Hero or Villian, Gender and Ability Type. This automatically flushes out and makes it easier for the player to change characters as they unlock more characters.
