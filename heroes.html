Heroes of Arak

Mobile User Interface Design

Objective

Design a mobile card game

Conceptual Idea

I designed and conceptualiazed this game for fun and out of boredom. The idea of this game is meant to battle in solo or in a team with other players and the user is suppose to bomb and capture the King hiding in the castles. The more diverse the character's abilities and skills are, the better chance you can win the game.

How to Win the Game

Once in battle, the player or team needs to find use their cards or powerups to destroy the enemy's base and find where the King is hiding. During the game, the player may wish to move the King to a different base with a special card in hand. Once the King is captured, the player or team wins the game.
