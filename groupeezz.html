Heroes of Arak

Mobile Game | UX Design

Overview

Groupeezz was a mobile app created to enhance the social movie experience for movie lovers to connect with others and discover unique entertainment experiences. On the app, movie lovers can connect, watch movies, and share their movie interests with their new buddies.

Role

We had a very small team, but I played a huge part in designing and creating promotional marketing materials. I worked very closely with the CEO and I developed a very good relationship with her. She heavily depends on and trusts my design creativity and skills to visually create her vision which we are usually aligned with.

In January 2017, we have transitioned to a different platform and decided to focus on Indie Filmmakers instead. We adopted a few business models from Groupeezz and will be incorpoated it into FilmHero's business model and plan.
